Ping Pong!

Enter a number greater than 1 and you'll get a sequence of numbers back that count up to that number. When the number in the sequence is divisible by 3, you'll see "ping" instead of that number. When the number is divisible by 5: "pong". If the number is divisible by both 3 and 5 (or 15 for that matter) you'll see "ping-pong". Start playing by entering a number below and clicking the PLAY! button. You can play as many times as you'd like! Just enter a new number and hit the PLAY! button again.
